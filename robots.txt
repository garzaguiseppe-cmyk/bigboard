# guiseppedrafts.net — crawler policy
# Ordinary search engines and user-requested AI assistants are welcome. Documented
# AI-training / bulk-dataset crawlers are asked to stay out. This expresses a preference;
# it is not a technical barrier and does not prevent scraping or create legal rights.
# Bot roles verified against each provider's official documentation (February 2026).

# Default — everything not named below is allowed (includes Googlebot, Bingbot, etc.).

# They do not collect training data, so they are treated like ordinary search.
