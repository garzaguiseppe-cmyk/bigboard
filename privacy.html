Privacy

Guiseppe Drafts
Effective 18 July 2026  ·  Last updated 18 July 2026

Guiseppe Drafts does not operate analytics, advertising, tracking pixels or application-level visitor logging. The site's mailto contact form does not transmit submissions to the website. GitHub Pages and visitors' email providers may process limited technical or email information as described below.

No cookies, no analytics, no tracking

Guiseppe Drafts is a static website. It does not use cookies, advertising, analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party embeds that profile you. Video clips are served from the site itself, not through third-party players. Guiseppe Drafts does not operate its own analytics or application logging. GitHub Pages may process standard technical request logs while delivering the site.

The contact form

The "Send a message" form on the Contact page does not send anything to this website or to any form-processing service. When you press Send, your browser opens your own email application with the message pre-filled and addressed to garzaguiseppe@gmail.com.

  • What it collects: the name, email and message you type are used only to build that pre-filled email in your own email app.
  • Who processes it: nothing is transmitted to or stored by this site. If you choose to send the email, it is delivered like any email you send — through your own email provider into the garzaguiseppe@gmail.com inbox, a Gmail account operated by Guiseppe Garza and hosted by Google.
  • Why: solely so you can contact the site's author.

You can also reach the author directly by email or via the linked social accounts. Those platforms operate under their own privacy policies.

Hosting and server logs

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub may automatically record standard technical request information (such as IP address and browser details) in server logs as part of delivering the site. That processing is carried out by GitHub under its own privacy practices, not by Guiseppe Drafts.

Retention

Because the site stores no personal data, there is nothing for it to retain. Any email you choose to send to garzaguiseppe@gmail.com is kept in that inbox at the author's discretion.

Access, correction and deletion

There is no personal data held by the site to access, correct or delete. If you have emailed garzaguiseppe@gmail.com and would like your message deleted, email that address to ask.
